What is Carbon Emission?

Carbon Emissions refer to the toxic carbon compounds into the atmosphere , primarily in the form of carbon dioxide(CO2) and other greenhouse gases(GHGs). These emissions result from human activites such as burning fossile fuels(coal , oil and natural gas) ,deforestation,industrial processes and various other activites. Carbon emissions are a major contributor to the enhaced greenhouse effect, leading to climate change and global warming.Especially from vehicles the carbon emission rate is found by 4.6 metric tons of CO2 per year.
